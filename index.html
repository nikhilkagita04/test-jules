The Problem with Polished Success Stories

The Gap in Traditional Narratives

Traditional media often highlights what successful people have done, showcasing their achievements. While inspiring, these accounts often focus on the thought process post-success and don't provide concrete, actionable items that listeners or readers can easily replicate. Survivorship bias means we primarily hear from the winners, missing valuable lessons from the everyday struggles and experiments of those still on their journey.

Our Solution: Real Journeys, Real Insights

We’re building a platform where founders, creators, and career climbers document their journeys as they unfold, sharing raw metrics, tactics, and struggles. No survivorship bias, no polished advice—just real people figuring it out, so you can learn and replicate their process in real-time.
